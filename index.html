FAQs

Shopify is a complete commerce platform that lets you start, grow, and manage a business. With Shopify, you can create and customize an online store, sell in multiple places, including web, mobile, social media, online marketplaces, brick-and-mortar locations, and pop-up shops, and manage products, inventory, payments, and shipping.

Shopify offers three main plans: Basic Shopify ($29/month), Shopify ($79/month), and Advanced Shopify ($299/month). Each plan includes different features and capabilities. There's also Shopify Lite for $9/month if you want to sell on social media or in person. You can try any plan free for 14 days with no credit card required.

Yes, you can use your own domain name with Shopify. You can either purchase a new domain through Shopify or connect an existing domain from another provider. Shopify makes it easy to set up and manage your domain directly from your admin dashboard.

No technical skills are needed to use Shopify. The platform is designed to be user-friendly with drag-and-drop tools, customizable templates, and intuitive interfaces. However, if you want advanced customization, you can hire a Shopify Expert or work with developers using Shopify's API and Liquid templating language.
